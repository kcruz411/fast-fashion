Clothing Brands For a Change

There are many clothing brands that aren't great for the environment. In these sites you'll come across clothes that is made to help solve this issue that is not well known.

WARNING! By clicking on these images and shopping you will make a great impact!

Real Fair Trade is their promise.Maggies organic cotton is from Chincha,Peru and they have achieved to get the GOTS certification. While aiming for environmental improvement through their socks and leggings they have also ensured the safety of their employees.

This line from Urban Outfitters uses unsold pieces from around the world and makes them into unique clothing pieces.

Prana offers fair trade certified garnments. Their clothing line varies from yoga wear to casual wear for men and women. Clothes made from recycled polyester, organic cotton, and recycled wool.
